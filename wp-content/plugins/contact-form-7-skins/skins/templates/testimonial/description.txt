Testimonial template allows you to gather the testimonies of your customers from a single form. You can customize the form as necessary.

Included in this template are the following fields:
• Title
• Tagline
• Radio buttons (for selecting a single option)
• Single line text area
• Multiple line text area
• Name field
• Email field
• Submit button

You can copy, add, remove and alter fields, text, titles and fieldsets and more to customize the form to suit your needs.
